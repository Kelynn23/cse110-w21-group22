    What is Pomodoro?

    The Pomodoro technique is a time management method where you break your work into chunks of tasks called "pomos" where each task will take you around 25 minutes to complete. It helps put your work and breaks into intervals so you can meaningfully get stuff done while not overworking yourself for long periods of time.


    How to use this Pomodoro timer

    1. Add tasks (also known as "pomos") to the task list.
    2. Start the timer and work for 25 minutes.
    3. After finishing your task or "pomo" take a break for 5 minutes.
    4. Get back to work! 25 minutes let's go!
    5. Hooray you can take a break! Relax for 15 minutes.

    Note: Safari users must disable "Stop Media with Sound" in the Preferences menu to get audio notifications.
